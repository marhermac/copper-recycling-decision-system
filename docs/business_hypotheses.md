# Business Hypotheses

## H1

Urban recyclers generate value primarily through labor-intensive material recovery.

## H2

Scrap dealers generate value through capital availability and material aggregation.

## H3

Wholesalers generate value through logistics and scale.

## H4

Foundries generate value through industrial transformation.

## H5

The profitability of copper recycling depends on three key factors:

* International copper prices.
* Inventory accumulation.
* Position within the supply chain.

## H6

Urban recyclers may increase profitability by optimizing the timing of their sales.
