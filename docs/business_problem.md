# Business Problem

## Observation

Every weekend, trucks collecting old appliances and scrap metal pass through my neighborhood in Argentina.

This observation led to a business question:

> How profitable is the copper recycling business?

## Business Objective

Develop a data-driven decision support system capable of determining whether an urban recycler should sell or accumulate copper based on market conditions.

## Key Questions

* How has the price of recycled copper evolved?
* How profitable is urban copper recycling?
* How does the copper supply chain work?
* When is the optimal time to sell?
* Can market trends improve decision-making?
